# Go Deeper

Curated sources behind loop engineering.

## Key Sources

- **Boris Cherny (Creator of Claude Code)**  
  Primary voice on shifting from prompting to loop engineering. Multiple interviews and posts about using `/loop`, `/goal`, verification, and running agents while sleeping.

- **Lenny's Podcast Interview with Boris Cherny (Feb 2026)**  
  One of the clearest explanations of his personal workflow (10-30 PRs/day, parallel agents, verification focus).

- **Addy Osmani**  
  Writes about agentic workflows and the shift in engineering roles.

- **Community Discussions**  
  Threads on hierarchical loops, builder + verifier separation, and durable state (`CLAUDE.md` + skills).

These patterns are what power the recipes in this repo.
